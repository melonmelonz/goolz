USAGE
  peek [scene] [flags]            run the eldritch tamagotchi TUI

CARE KEYS
  f   feed it a question         t   tend (steady the tether)
  r   read a chapter together    z   drill (procedurally generated)
  w   wander (text adventure: 10 rooms, 16 items, find rare drops)

WANDER KEYS
  n s e w   move (or arrow keys)    t   take the room's pending drop
  l       look                    h   listen (atmospheric prose)
  c       call out to your creature
  1-9     use an inventory slot   q   leave the field

SHOW OFF
  d   demo (cycle stages, moods, mutations — doesn't save your run)
  i   replay the intro (multi-beat: void, sighting, rules, naming)

META
  ?   help                       Q   quit (closes the native CLI)

HOW IT WORKS
  three stats decay on a half-life model: nourishment, tether, lucidity.
  feed correct answers, tend the creature, and read together to keep it
  alive. neglect it and it goes. progression gates on recalls + chapters
  read + average stat — keep showing up and your creature evolves.

PERSISTENCE
  state lives in your browser (localStorage). use [ reset ] in the title bar
  to clear state and memorials. the native CLI runs the same scenes from a
  single static-musl binary.
