the demo scene snapshots your creature on entry and restores it on exit unless you press Enter, so you can show off without disturbing your run.

how it works

three stats decay on a half-life model: nourishment, tether, lucidity. feed correct answers, tend the creature, and read together to keep it alive. neglect it and it goes.

state lives in your browser (localStorage). the native CLI runs the same scenes from a single static-musl binary.
