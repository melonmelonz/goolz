④ Notes From Doing It

  • Saving + refreshing after every line really does help — you watch the page assemble itself piece by piece.
  • I have emmet-vim sitting in my plugins; it would expand ! + Ctrl-y,, into the whole skeleton. Didn't load it. Typing's the point.
  • Indentation isn't required by HTML, but the page felt instantly more readable once I matched indents to nesting depth.
  • An <img> with a broken src just shows the alt text plus a placeholder icon — alt text isn't optional, it's the safety net.
  • The <a> link to Goonhammer is blue + underlined by default. Same color as the page from Ex 1 — that's the browser's user-agent stylesheet, not anything I did.
